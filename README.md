# 📊 Frissly Market Intelligence & Growth Strategy Brief

A comprehensive market research and growth strategy report for **Frissly**, an organic food startup focused on providing fresh, chemical-free, and certified organic food products.

This project combines **market research, competitive intelligence, digital marketing audit, customer insights, SWOT analysis, and strategic recommendations** into an interactive HTML report.

---

## 📌 Project Overview

The objective of this project was to evaluate Frissly's current market position and identify actionable growth opportunities through data-driven business analysis.

The report includes:

- Market Research
- Industry Analysis
- Digital Presence Audit
- Competitor Benchmarking
- Customer Persona Analysis
- SWOT Analysis
- Growth Opportunities
- Revenue Expansion Ideas
- 90-Day Strategic Roadmap
- Key Business KPIs

---

## 🎯 Business Objectives

- Understand Frissly's competitive position
- Evaluate digital marketing performance
- Identify growth bottlenecks
- Analyze customer acquisition opportunities
- Improve customer retention
- Increase Average Order Value (AOV)
